## 💡 RESPONSES TO COMMON QUESTIONS

### **"Why do I need a chatbot? My phone works fine."**

**Response:** "Right, but 40% of people prefer NOT to call. They want to chat. The AI handles common questions instantly, and you only get warm leads ready to buy. It also answers at 2 AM when families are scared and searching online."

### **"This looks too fancy/expensive."**

**Response:** "The trial is free. And it's actually cheaper than your competitors pay. Most agencies spend $2,000-$5,000/month on marketing. We're $500-$1,500. The design looks nice, but the real value is the leads and efficiency."

### **"I already have a website."**

**Response:** "Exactly. But it's not ranking well, not converting visitors, and it doesn't have 24/7 AI support. We're upgrading what you have, not replacing it from scratch. Your WordPress site becomes a lead-generation machine."

### **"How do I know this will actually work?"**

**Response:** "In 30 days, we show you the results: phone inquiries, website traffic, chatbot conversations, etc. We track everything and report monthly. If results don't materialize, we adjust strategy at no cost. But based on what we're doing, we're confident it will work."

### **"Can I see examples of other clients?"**

**Response:** "This is our first home care trial, but we've done website redesigns for [insert examples if any]. The strategy is proven in the industry. Want to do a 30-minute call so Nova can walk through the exact plan?"

### **"I don't like the dark theme."**

**Response:** "We can adjust colors. This is just the mockup. But look — every modern SaaS company uses dark themes. It's professional. And it stands out vs. your competitors' generic light blue sites."
