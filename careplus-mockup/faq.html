Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our services and care options

About Our Services

What areas do you serve in Milwaukee?

+

We provide care throughout Milwaukee and surrounding areas including Shorewood, Wauwatosa, Brookfield, Elm Grove, Waukesha, Menomonee Falls, and more. If you're not sure if we serve your area, chat with our AI assistant or call us at (414) 585-0083.

Do you offer 24/7 care?

+

Yes! We offer overnight care, live-in care, and round-the-clock arrangements. Our flexible scheduling allows us to match your family's specific needs, whether that's a few hours a week or full-time 24/7 support.

Are your caregivers background-checked and trained?

+

Absolutely. All our caregivers undergo thorough background checks, drug screening, and reference verification. Many are certified nursing assistants (CNAs) or have specialized training in dementia care, post-surgery care, and more.

Is your care RN supervised?

+

Yes. All care plans are developed and overseen by licensed Registered Nurses (RNs). This ensures medical safety and personalized care that adapts to your loved one's changing health needs.

Pricing & Insurance

How much does home care cost?

+

Rates vary based on service type:

• Personal Care: $25-$35/hour
• Overnight Care: $150-$250/night
• Live-In Care: $2,500-$4,500/month
• Dementia Care: $30-$50/hour

These are average ranges. Schedule a free assessment to get a personalized quote for your situation.

Do you accept insurance or Medicaid?

+

We work with several insurance plans and Medicaid programs. However, many families use private pay for flexibility and choice. Our care specialists can discuss payment options and help you explore benefits you may qualify for. Call us to discuss your specific situation.

Is there a minimum number of hours I need to book?

+

We're flexible! Minimum hours vary depending on the service. Some families start with just a few hours per week for companionship or light help, while others need full-time care. Our specialists will work with you to create an arrangement that fits your needs and budget.

Getting Started

How do I schedule a free assessment?

+

It's easy! You can:

• Call us: (414) 585-0083
• Use our AI chat: Available 24/7 on the homepage
• Fill out a contact form: We'll reach out within 2 hours

During the assessment, we'll understand your loved one's needs, discuss care options, and provide a personalized care plan.

How quickly can you start providing care?

+

In most cases, we can begin care within 3-5 business days. For urgent situations (post-hospital discharge, sudden caregiving need), we may be able to arrange care even faster. Ask about our emergency care options.

Can I meet my caregiver before they start?

+

Yes! We believe the relationship between your loved one and their caregiver is crucial. Before care begins, you'll meet your assigned caregiver and have the opportunity to ensure it's a good fit. If you'd prefer a different caregiver, we can arrange that.

What if I'm not satisfied with the care?

+

Your satisfaction is our priority. If you're not happy with your caregiver or care plan, contact us immediately. We'll work to resolve the issue or arrange a different caregiver at no additional cost. We're committed to your loved one's well-being.

Specialized Care

Do you have caregivers trained in dementia care?

+

Yes. We have specialized caregivers trained in Alzheimer's and dementia care. They understand behavioral challenges, memory loss, and use evidence-based techniques to provide comfort and safety. We offer both part-time and live-in dementia care options.

Can you help with post-surgery recovery?

+

Absolutely. Our post-surgery care includes wound care support, mobility assistance, medication management, and coordination with your healthcare team. We help ensure smooth recovery right in your home.

Do you provide care for veterans?

+

Yes! We specialize in veteran care and understand the unique needs and benefits available to veterans. Ask us about VA benefits, Aid & Attendance programs, and veteran-specific support.
