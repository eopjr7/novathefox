Our Care Services

Comprehensive home care solutions tailored to your family's needs

🛁 Personal Care Services

Our caregivers provide compassionate assistance with daily living activities, helping your loved one maintain dignity and independence.

Includes:
Bathing, dressing, grooming, toileting, medication reminders, meal prep, light housekeeping
Availability:
Hourly, daily, or full-time arrangements
Cost Range:
$25-$35/hour (depends on service level)

🧠 Alzheimer's & Dementia Care

Specialized care for clients with memory loss and cognitive decline. Our trained caregivers use proven techniques to provide comfort and safety.

Includes:
Behavioral support, memory activities, safety monitoring, structured routines, family counseling
Availability:
24-hour care, live-in options available
Cost Range:
$30-$50/hour (specialized training included)

🌙 Overnight Care Services

Professional overnight companions provide peace of mind for families needing nighttime support and monitoring.

Includes:
Overnight monitoring, fall prevention, medication assistance, mobility help, emergency response
Duration:
10 PM - 6 AM (or custom schedule)
Cost Range:
$150-$250 per night

🏠 Live-In Care

Full-time, in-home care with a dedicated caregiver living with your loved one. Perfect for those needing 24/7 support.

Includes:
All personal care, meal prep, medication management, transportation, housekeeping, companionship
Duration:
24/7 live-in arrangement
Cost Range:
$2,500-$4,500/month (depends on location & care needs)

🏥 Post-Hospital & Recovery Care

Specialized care during recovery from surgery or illness. We work with your healthcare team to ensure proper rehabilitation.

Includes:
Wound care support, mobility assistance, physical therapy coordination, medical equipment help, pain management
Availability:
Short-term or long-term arrangements
Cost Range:
$28-$40/hour

❤️ Companion Care & Engagement

Social engagement and companionship to reduce isolation and support mental well-being. Often the most valued service by families.

Includes:
Conversation & engagement, outings & errands, hobby activities, appointment transportation, social support
Availability:
Flexible hourly or daily schedules
Cost Range:
$20-$30/hour
